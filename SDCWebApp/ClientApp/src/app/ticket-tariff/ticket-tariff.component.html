Everybody love discounts

For childs

Each child who visits SDC brings with them a great deal of knowledge about the glass heritage. Therefore, one of the main discounts in SDC is addressed to children.

For seniors

Seniors often do not sit at home at all. instead they travel, visit, get to know new places and people. for such people, the SDC team has prepared discounts for visiting the pre-penetrated cellars and the Glass Heritage Centre.

For groups

Why not go on a sightseeing tour with friends, family or a partner? No matter who you go with, there will always be a discount for tickets for organized groups.
