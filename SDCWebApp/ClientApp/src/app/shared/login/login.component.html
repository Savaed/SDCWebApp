Welcome to SDC Platform

The SDC Platform is a place where every moderator or administrator can manage all types of resources available on the main SDC website. From informative articles, through ticket pricelists, to information on visiting museums, such as the maximum allowed group size and even the maximum age of the customer who can get a discount for the child.

You do not have access to the platform? This system is only for SDC employees who have registered accounts.
