The SDC museum

The Glass Heritage Center is Poland's first tourist and cultural facility in an interactive way presenting the subject of glass industry and glass works.

Informative lectures

Our visitors can not only admire the beauty of glass art and the craftsmanship of glass artists but above all learn the secrets of production and decorative techniques in which they can take an active part.

Perfect visit duration

Visiting both the main Center building and basements takes about 2 hours and the main building itself about 1.5 hours. The tour takes place under the guidance of a guide.

Two buildings

We offer an amazing journey through the world of glass. Our Center consists of two facilities - the main building located at Blich 2 street and the cellars, connect in front of the western frontage of the Krosno Market Square.

Workhouse shows

See artistic glassmaking

During spectacular shows, artistic and decorative objects are created in front of the viewers. You can also try your hand and blow out the glass mold yourself

Have you never seen glass workshop shows? See how specialists from SDC do it.

Atractions

Artactions for everyone

See amazing glass art shows, learn more about the glass itself and visit exhibitions of the greatest artists from Poland and abroad.

The cellars

The cellars contain, above all, an exhibition of the most valuable works of glass art made by the most famous artists working or associated with Krosno.

The basement

In the basement of the Krosno market you can also see examples of glass made in the city for famous world brands, including Venetian Murano.

Workshops

Glass processing and decorating workshops: glass painting workshop, engraving workshop, production of stained glass and production of glass figurines using the burner method.

Visit and see

The SDC is a facility that presents the glass industry in a completely new formula - modern, attractive and affordable, thanks to which the multidimensional world of glass becomes extremely close.
