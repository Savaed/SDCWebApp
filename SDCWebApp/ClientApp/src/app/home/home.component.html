Glass heritage of Krosno

Thanks to the achievements of modern museology and modern techniques, the Glass Heritage Center not only tells the story of for decades in Poland and the world of Krosno glass, but also creates it.

Visit us and see the breathtaking shows

The most important attractions of the Center are interactive shows of glass production and decoration, in which tourists can take an active part.

Exhibitions in SDC

Visit exhibitions of the greatest artists

In the basement of the pre-door and in the room of temporary exhibitions, however, you can get acquainted with exhibitions presenting both artistic glass, made by the best Polish artists.

Glass tour

GlassTour, a traveling glassworks, is a project thanks to which metallurgical shows can visit anywhere in Poland! The small metallurgical furnace allows for the organization of outdoor shows, during which viewers will not only see how glass is formed, but will have the opportunity to try the art of blowing it out themselves.
